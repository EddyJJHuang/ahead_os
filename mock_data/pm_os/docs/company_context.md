## What we do
Meridian is a B2B commerce platform. Customers run their storefronts and checkout on us. Revenue mix is moving from SMB to enterprise accounts with six-figure ARR.

## Operating norms
- Launches go through a Go/No-Go review with explicit criteria.
- P0 = production-blocking or revenue/data-integrity risk; P0s in payments block launches.
- QA sign-off is mandatory for any payment-path change.
- Communication to affected enterprise accounts precedes customer-visible launches.
