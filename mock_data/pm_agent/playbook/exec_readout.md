# Executive Readout Playbook

Use this playbook when writing for a VP, GM, founder, or cross-functional launch review. The output should be short, decision-oriented, and evidence-backed.

## Structure

Decision:
One sentence with Go, Conditional Go, or Hold.

Confidence:
Percentage plus one sentence explaining confidence.

Why now:
Three bullets max. Each bullet must include a concrete evidence ID.

Customer exposure:
Named accounts, segment, revenue at risk, deadline, and customer ask.

Engineering blockers:
Only include blockers that change the launch decision. Cite Jira keys, PR IDs, and failing checks.

What changed:
Call out new information since the last review when timestamps exist.

Decision needed:
What leadership must approve, unblock, or accept.

Next update:
Time/date and owner for the next status update.

## Tone

Be calm, direct, and specific. Do not use vague optimism. Do not say "mostly ready" when launch criteria are unmet. Do not hide uncertainty in narrative prose.
