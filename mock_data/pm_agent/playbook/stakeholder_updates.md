# Stakeholder Updates Playbook

Stakeholder updates should be short, decisive, and evidence-backed. A launch update should not hide risk in prose. Use direct status labels and make asks visible.

## Launch Update Format

Decision: Go, conditional go, or hold.

Why: one to three facts that determine the decision.

Customer impact: named accounts, ARR exposure when available, and the message they need.

Owners and dates: next action, owner, and due date for each blocker.

Ask: the decision or help needed from leadership.

## Tone

Be calm and specific. Avoid blame. Use "blocked by", "waiting on", and "next evidence" language. Do not mark an item green because it is close; mark it green only when the evidence exists.
