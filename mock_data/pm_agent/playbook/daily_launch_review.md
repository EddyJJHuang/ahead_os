# Daily Launch Review Workflow

Use this workflow for questions like "What changed since yesterday?", "What should I do today?", or "Are we still on track?"

## Inputs

- Jira issues with updated_at, status, priority, blocker flags, owners, and due dates.
- GitHub PRs with status, checks, reviews, and linked Jira keys.
- Customer escalations with account, severity, revenue, and deadline.
- Email and Slack messages with customer or executive asks.
- Calendar events with launch review, QA sign-off, customer calls, and exec readouts.
- Tasks with owner, due date, and completion status.

## Steps

1. Identify new blockers or blockers that got worse.
2. Identify blockers that are resolved or newly validated.
3. Compare due dates against the launch date and today's date.
4. Pull customer escalations and revenue exposure.
5. Check if owners are named for each critical action.
6. Decide today's PM priorities.
7. Produce a stakeholder-ready update.

## Output

Today's PM focus:

What changed:

Top launch risks:

Customer follow-ups:

Owner gaps:

Decisions needed today:

Next update:

Do not include low-priority work unless it affects the launch decision.
